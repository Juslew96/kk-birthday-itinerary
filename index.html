KK's 1st Birthday Itinerary

Newport Beach, California
May 21 – 26, 2026 · The Lewis Family & Grandparents
11:00 AM — Arrive at John Wayne Airport
Pick up the Lincoln Navigator from the rental counter. SNA is small and easy — you'll be out quickly.
18601 Airport Way, Santa Ana, CA 92707
11:30 AM – 1:30 PM — Grocery Run & Lunch
Stock the Villa Kitchen for the Week
Option A: Walmart Supercenter (Recommended)
10 min from airport · 12 min to resort · Open 6 AM – 11 PM daily
16555 Von Karman Ave, Irvine, CA 92606
Option B: Costco Wholesale
12 min from airport · 15 min to resort · Open 10 AM – 8:30 PM (Thu)
2700 Park Ave #1001B, Tustin, CA 92782
Grab lunch at In-N-Out Burger — a California must-try.
In-N-Out: 3810 Barranca Pkwy, Irvine
Tip: Villas have full kitchens — stovetop, oven, dishwasher, full-size fridge. Cooking breakfast in saves time and money with a big group.
2:00 PM – 4:00 PM — Head to Resort & Unwind
Marriott's Newport Coast Villas
Unload groceries, relax, explore the resort — 5 heated pools, splash pad, fire pits, putting green, ocean views. Check-in at 4:00 PM.
23000 Newport Coast Dr, Newport Coast, CA 92657
5:00 PM – 7:30 PM — Sunset Dinner
Beachcomber Café at Crystal Cove
Iconic oceanfront restaurant on the sand. Arrive by 5 PM for the daily Martini Flag raising ceremony at sunset. Seafood-forward, great cocktails at The Bootlegger tiki bar.
15 Crystal Cove, Newport Beach, CA 92657
Tip: Reserve at thebeachcombercafe.com. Request patio seating. Take the resort shuttle or park at Los Trancos lot ($15/vehicle).
7:30 – 8:30 AM — Breakfast & Depart
Quick villa breakfast. Pack diaper bag, stroller, snacks, sunscreen, layers. Depart by 8:30 AM. ~1 hr 20 min via I-5 South.
Nap strategy: She'll fall asleep ~10 AM in the car. Arrive at the zoo ~9:50–10:00 AM. Let her finish her nap in the stroller while adults ride the Guided Bus Tour.
Tip: Each couple takes their own vehicle for flexibility.
~10:00 AM — Arrive at San Diego Zoo
San Diego Zoo — Balboa Park
Hours: Opens 9 AM · Tickets: Buy 1-Day Pass online at zoo.sandiegozoo.org · Kids 2 & under: FREE · Parking: $16/vehicle
2920 Zoo Drive, San Diego, CA 92108
11:30 AM – 2:30 PM — Prime Zoo Time
Wildlife Explorers Basecamp — 3+ acres of interactive play, perfect for a 1-year-old. Walk the highlights: polar bears, elephants, giraffes, koalas, Panda Ridge. Lunch at Albert's or grab-and-go. Skyfari Aerial Tram for views.
Tip: Download the San Diego Zoo app. Bring a stroller that handles hills. Friday is much less crowded than Saturday of Memorial Day weekend.
2:30 PM — Head to the Car
Nap strategy: Wrap up by 2:30 PM. She'll catch her 2:45 PM nap on the 1 hr 20 min drive home.
5:00 PM – Evening — Resort Night
Relax after a big day. Cook dinner in the villa or order from La Vista Pool Bar & Grill (on-site). Fire pits after the baby goes down.
8:00 – 9:30 AM — Villa Breakfast
Relaxed breakfast at the villa.
9:30 AM — Drive to Balboa Island
Balboa Island & Fun Zone
Stroll Marine Ave, grab a frozen chocolate banana from Sugar 'n Spice. Browse the shops.
300 Marine Ave, Balboa Island, CA 92662
10:00 AM — Nap #1: Baby naps in the stroller on the flat, quiet boardwalk while adults explore.
11:30 AM – 12:30 PM — Ferry & Fun Zone
Take the Balboa Island Ferry ($2/person) to the Peninsula. Ferris wheel, arcade, shops at the Fun Zone. Lunch at Ruby's Diner on Balboa Pier.
Balboa Fun Zone: 600 E Bay Ave, Balboa, CA 92661
1:00 – 2:30 PM — Duffy Boat Cruise
Duffy Electric Boat — Newport Harbor
Rent for 1.5–2 hrs. Seats 12 — whole group fits. Cruise past mansions, mega yachts, sea lions. Easy to drive (5 mph). Bring snacks & drinks.
Tip: Book at duffyboats.com. ~$150–200/hr. Must be 21+ to rent. Life jackets provided for all ages.
2:30 PM — Head Back
2:45 PM — Nap #2: She naps in the car on the drive back (20 min). Adults freshen up for dinner.
5:30 – 8:00 PM — Special Dinner
RH Ocean Grill — Fashion Island
Rooftop dining, 4th floor of RH. Glass atrium, olive trees, chandeliers, fountain, coastal views. Seafood & caviar, hearth classics. Smart casual.
Hours: Sat 11 AM – 10 PM · Reserve: OpenTable
Budget: $$$ (Entrées $35–65)
1101 Newport Center Dr, Newport Beach, CA 92660
Tip: Book ASAP — hard to get. After dinner, stroll Fashion Island under the string lights.
7:30 – 9:30 AM — Early Beach Morning
Little Corona del Mar Beach & Tide Pools
Calm waters, tide pools, no-stairs access. Baby plays in the sand, grandparents explore tide pools.
Ocean Blvd & Poppy Ave, Corona del Mar, CA 92625
Tip: Arrive early for parking. Bring pop-up shade tent. Low tide = best tide pools.
9:45 AM — Back to Resort
10:00 AM — Nap #1: Villa nap. Adults enjoy pool or relax on the balcony with coffee.
11:30 AM – 2:30 PM — Crystal Cove & Laguna Beach
Explore Crystal Cove & Stroll Laguna Beach
Walk the Crystal Cove Historic District (1930s cottages, bluff trails). Then drive to Laguna Beach for Heisler Park — flat, scenic, stroller-friendly. Lunch in downtown Laguna.
Heisler Park: 375 Cliff Dr, Laguna Beach, CA 92651
2:30 PM — Back to Resort
2:45 PM — Nap #2: Villa nap. Adults rest and get ready for Nobu.
5:30 – 8:00 PM — Nobu Dinner
Nobu Newport Beach — Lido Marina Village
Two-story waterfront Japanese restaurant. Yellowtail Jalapeño, Black Cod Miso, Omakase tasting menu. Harbor views, indoor/outdoor dining.
Hours: Sun 12–3 PM, 5–9 PM · Reserve: noburestaurants.com/newportbeach
Budget: $$$$ ($80–150/person) · 5+ guests require CC hold
3450 Via Oporto, Newport Beach, CA 92663
After dinner, stroll Lido Marina Village — boutiques, galleries, harbor views.
8:00 – 9:30 AM — Villa Breakfast
Leisurely breakfast. No rush. Coffee on the balcony with ocean views one last full day.
9:30 AM — Splash Pad & Pool
Head to the Coast Pool with the splash pad (open 10 AM – sunset). Baby plays in the water before nap.
10:00 AM — Nap #1: Back to the villa. Grandparents enjoy pool, spa, or resort grounds.
11:30 AM – 2:30 PM — Pool & Resort Time
Back to the pool! Lunch from La Vista Pool Bar & Grill (on-site). Putting green, game room, bluff trail walk.
2:30 PM
2:45 PM — Nap #2: Villa nap. Adults rest, start packing, freshen up.
3:30 – 5:30 PM — Fashion Island Shopping
Fashion Island
100+ stores, ocean views, koi ponds, fountains, string lights. Nordstrom, Bloomingdale's, luxury boutiques. Free parking.
401 Newport Center Dr, Newport Beach, CA 92660
6:00 – 8:00 PM — Final Night Dinner
Last Supper on the Coast
Javier's — Upscale Mexican, beautiful patio, 5 min from resort
Mastro's Ocean Club — Steakhouse, ocean views, dressy
R+D Kitchen — Fashion Island, elevated American
Villa BBQ — Cook on the resort grill, gather around the fire pit
Javier's: 7832 E Coast Hwy, Newport Beach Mastro's: 8112 E Coast Hwy, Newport Beach
6:00 AM — Wake Up & Pack
Early morning. Coffee in the villa, one last ocean view from the balcony.
7:00 AM — Check Out
Load luggage. 15-minute drive to SNA.
7:30 AM — Arrive at John Wayne Airport
Return rental car, check in. SNA is compact — 1.5 hrs before departure is plenty.
18601 Airport Way, Santa Ana, CA 92707
Tip: Return Navigator with full tank. Gas on Jamboree Rd near the airport. TSA is typically fast at SNA.
9:00 AM — Wheels Up ✈️
Head home with a camera roll full of memories.
